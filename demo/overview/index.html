Definitions


Merchant
The business or entity who pays for and launchesan adveritsing campaign

Publisher
The website, app, or platform that displays ads delivered by Asttro

User
The individual that interacts with ads on the Publisher platform and later transacts with the Merchant

Product Overview


Step 1
Merchant launches a campaign about their products or services through Asttro

Step 2
User visits Publisher site that has integrated Asttro ads

Step 3
User interacts with ads on Publisher site

Step 4
User transacts with same Merchant whose ad they interacted with in Step 3

Step 5
Transaction is recognized and Publisher is rewarded for causing the transaction
