First step: Merchant launches a campaign
Overview
Merchants create, launch and manage advertising campaigns through the Attro dashboard. To create a campaign, Merchants upload all creative information that is to be displayed about the Ad in addition to transaction information and budget.
Instructions
In this step, you will act as the Merchant who is going to create a new Campaign. Fill out all of the information and launch the Campaign before continuing to the next step.
